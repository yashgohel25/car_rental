    The vehicle must be returned to the agreed-upon location at the end of the rental period.

  9. Condition

    The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as documented during the initial inspection and videography.

  10. Charges

    Payment is to be made according to the pre-agreed kilometers. Any additional kilometers driven will be charged extra.

  11. Legality

    Car rentals fall under the Motor Vehicle Act Section 43 & 51.

  16. Mileage & Time

    Please verify the starting and closing kilometers and time before and after your rental period.

    We charge for a second day after 12:00 PM.

    Alcohol and smoking materials are prohibited in the vehicle.

    Advance deposits are non-refundable under any circumstances.
